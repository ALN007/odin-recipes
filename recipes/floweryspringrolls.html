Flowery Spring Rolls

Description

A fresh and colorful meal that is a great feature appetizer or fancy snack for the gardener in all of us

pairs well with crunchy or smooth peanut sauce made simply with less than 5 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • finely sliced radishes
  • nastrium flowers
  • violets
  • rice paper
  • assorted vegetables: carrots, cucumbers, daikon

Steps:

  1. clean and finely slice vegetables
  2. dip rice paper into water completely (use bowl)
  3. place ingredients on rice paper, flowers facing down first and remaining ingredients on top
  4. roll the ends in one inch, and roll longitudinally until it resembles picture
