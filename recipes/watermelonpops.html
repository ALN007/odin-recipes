Watermelon Pops

Description

Another great snack on the go or to sit and savor on those sweltering hot days

May spice it up for the tajin lover in all of us by topping it by none other than Tajin!

Ingredients

  • watermelon
  • kiwis
  • popsicle sticks

Steps

  1. core and blend the meat of the watermelon
  2. peel and blend meat of kiwis
  3. place watermelong mix in molds to freeze first, with popsicle stick in place
  4. later add kiwi mix, after 30 min of initial freeze
