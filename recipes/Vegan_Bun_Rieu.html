Vegan Bun Rieu

Description

A warm and tomato-based soup great in the hot or cold weather. Goodies such as tofu, large diced tomatoes, and rice noodles swim in this soup topped with fresh lemon grass, chopped cabbage, and thai basil.

This recipe only takes a few hours and can be prepared while wathing Silicon Valley!

Ingredients

  • 800 g daikon
  • 500 g pear
  • 900 g chayotes
  • 1 stalk lemon grass
  • 250 g carrots
  • 2 L coconut water
  • 5 L water
  • 2 cups lion's mane mushroom

Steps

  1. chop vegetables in cubes
  2. prepare wet ingredients in stock pot
  3. add chopped vegetables and stew for 40-50 min
